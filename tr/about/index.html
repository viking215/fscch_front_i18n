About the Foundation

Reporting

The Fund was registered by Rosatom State Corporation on December 23, 2019, according to the decision of the sole founder dated December 19, 2019.

The objective of the Fund is the formation of property on the basis of voluntary contributions, and other not prohibited by law receipts for the implementation of socially useful initiatives, programmes and projects for the maintenance, reproduction, protection and promotion of Christian values of the culture and heritage of the religious, aimed at solving the following tasks:

  • the promotion of religious culture, art, science, education, moral education and personality development that is necessary in the public interest;
  • providing free of charge to legal entities and individuals resources for the implementation of initiatives, programs and projects aimed at achieving the statutory goals of the Fund on the terms of providing the Fund with a report on the targeted use of the resources received;
  • promotion of peace, friendship and harmony between peoples, prevention of social, national and religious conflicts;
  • development of interethnic cooperation, preservation and protection of the identity of spiritual traditions and religious culture of different peoples.
